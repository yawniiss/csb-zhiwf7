About zkGhost?

Built on the zkSync network, zkGhost is a community-owned fair launch token. The project initiative seeks to familiarize individuals with the zkSync ecosystem using ghosts that represent people feeling lonely in their lives. The goal of zkGhost would be to provide a community for individuals who feel isolated or disconnected, where they can connect with others who share similar interests and experiences. The token would be used to access exclusive content, participate in community events, and receive discounts on products and services related to the theme of the community.


Overall, zkGhost would serve as a platform for individuals to connect with others, access exclusive benefits, and support charitable causes related to loneliness and isolation. The token would provide a means of access to these benefits and serve as a way to support the community's mission.

How does it work?

The protocol is based on a series of complex interactions, with the behavior of multiple stakeholders deciding the pricing dynamics of the multiple tokens of the ecosystem.

When ATomb price is above the peg, new ATomb is minted through the protocol to inflate the supply, in an attempt to drive the price down towards the peg. The new ATomb tokens are placed into circulation through the Crypt and distributed to ASHARE holders. This increases the demand for ASHARE, thus increasing the price and value of ASHARE tokens.

When ATomb price drops below the peg, the protocol will allow minting ABonds with ATomb (up to a maximum debt limit). This removes Atomb from the total supply, applying upward pressure on the price towards the peg. ABonds can then be redeemed for ATomb at a premium when the price is above peg in the future.

Why is ‘at-peg’ (TWAP between 1.00 and 1.01) the best time to provide liquidity?

When ATomb is pegged or close to being pegged to 2,000 ATomb: 1 ETH, it is more akin to having exposure to a single asset (single staking) than to your traditional LP-ing experience, where you would run the risk of impermanent loss if one of the tokens went up in value and the other did not.

Wait, you mean that if ETH pumps, ATomb pumps with it?

Yes! Let’s take an example: If ETH increases in price, it won’t ‘outrun’ ATomb. The APR will vary in terms of its USD value, but emissions won’t. This is something that wouldn’t be possible with another 1:1 pegged asset like a stable coin LP position, where the USD value is directly tied to the emissions. If ETH rises in USD value, ATomb goes with it. Same if ETH falls in USD value, ATomb will be worth less in USD, but it won’t affect the peg. Peg is solely determined based on buying and selling volume of ATomb.

What is the formula to calculate the redemption bonus for EBOND?

To encourage redemption of ABOND forATomb when ATomb’s TWAP > 1.1, and in order to incentivize users to redeem at a higher price, ABOND redemption will be more profitable with a higher ATomb TWAP value.

What happens if I interact with the Crypt in any way?

Any interaction with the Crypt will reset both timers. That’s 3 epochs (18 hours) to withdraw your ATomb rewards, and 6 epochs to unstake your ASHARE (36 hours).
